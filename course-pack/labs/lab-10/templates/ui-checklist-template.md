## Instructions

Use this checklist to audit your capstone's UI/UX. Focus on **functional clarity** not visual beauty.

**Standard:** Consistent, clear, doesn't distract from innovation

**NOT the standard:** Award-winning design, beautiful graphics, professional polish

## What NOT to Worry About

Remember, these are NOT required for this course:
- ❌ Beautiful visual design
- ❌ Custom illustrations or icons
- ❌ Smooth animations or transitions
- ❌ Mobile responsive design (desktop is fine)
- ❌ Dark mode
- ❌ Advanced accessibility features (beyond basics)
- ❌ Cross-browser testing
- ❌ Pixel-perfect alignment

**Focus on: Clarity, Consistency, and Functionality**
